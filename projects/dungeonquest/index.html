DungeonQuest board game

This project involved creating a workable software model that could represent DungeonQuest, a fantasy-role-playing board game.
This involved creating modelling diagrams, reviewing our work and work of others, discussions on the decisions in the modelled design, documents detailing risk factors and planning for development, and a Java skeleton of the proposed application.

  • Technologies: UML, Java, Eclipse IDE
  • Course: Advanced Software Design / Avancerad programvarudesign
    @ Uppsala University, Sweden
  • Role: Project Manager, Workhorse
  • Year: Fall 2013


Taking hold on this project developed experience in software design, especially toward the design of object-oriented systems – from analysis of requirements to low level design (classes, their relationships and methods).

Content covered was the application of design principles, best-practices, design and architecture patterns and UML modeling notation.

Documents are split between Google Drive and github (intentionally, to use draw.io)
